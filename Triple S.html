Welcome to Triple S

Who are we?

Smiley Smoothies and Skates aka Triple S is both a juice bar and a skating rink, the place for all the fun you need.

We are the number one place for you to roll your worries away and laugh your heart out over a chilled smoothie with fam and friends both physically and virtually.

What makes us different?

We are dedicated to giving you the best smoothies, fruit juices and roller skating experience with a focus on uniqueness, customer service and fun.

Then and now!

Founded in 2021 by Lys Ilunga, Triple S has come a long way, from its begennings in Lys's grandma's kitchen where she started selling smoothies to her siblings and now serve in many locations across the city near high schools and tertiary education institutions for the youth to have a relaxed place to escape the daily stress.

We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you
In case there is something you would like to know, please head over to our social media platforms and all you need to know will be provided to you.
