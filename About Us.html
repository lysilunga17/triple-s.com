Our story ...

It had always been all about the love of a banana based smoothie recipe that the founder, Lys and her siblings enjoyed like crazy
As a girl she was always waiting for the time all the family friends would gather under the night sky around a table filled with glasses full of the very tasty smoothies her mama made but the excitement was mostly for the jokes and laughter that accompanied those chilled moments as the climax was the fight over who will have what will be left in the blender.
She then decided that when she grows up, eveybody would have a taste of the legendary "Jus de Banane".

The rink came about from a show based on a group of teenagers passionate about roller skating that she really enjoyed, even though she was not practicing the sport.
Believing that nothing is impossible she mastered the courage to put on a pair of roller skates, fell a couple of times, actually, many times but finally managed to stand without losing her balance until she became an above average roller skater.

The Triple S was designed for you to cool down with a glass of chilled whatever after a long exercise or warm yourself with a hot drink or fill your tummy after taking spins for fun, or maybe just meet up with friends to socialize with beautiful music in the background to accompany the chats that will be awesome memories in future.

Out of an ambitious dreamer, the Triple S came to existence.
